About the Book

How Not to Diet: The Groundbreaking Science of Healthy, Permanent Weight Loss is a revolutionary guide that debunks common weight loss myths and presents evidence-based strategies for achieving sustainable, healthy weight management. Unlike fad diets that promise quick fixes, this book focuses on long-term lifestyle changes backed by rigorous scientific research.

Written by Dr. Michael Greger, founder of NutritionFacts.org, this comprehensive book synthesizes thousands of nutrition studies to provide clear, actionable advice on weight loss. It addresses why most diets fail and presents a science-based approach to losing weight without counting calories or feeling deprived.

This book isn't about restrictive dieting—it's about understanding the biological mechanisms behind weight gain and learning how to work with your body's natural systems to achieve and maintain a healthy weight for life.
