Donation FAQ

Is my donation secure?

Yes, all donations are processed through PayPal's secure payment system. We never store your payment information.

Can I donate anonymously?

Yes, PayPal allows you to make anonymous donations. Your privacy is important to us.

Will I get a receipt?

Yes, PayPal will automatically email you a receipt for your donation for tax purposes.

Are donations tax-deductible?

We're working on obtaining 501(c)(3) non-profit status. Currently, donations are not tax-deductible but help support our mission immensely.
