About the Book

Thinking, Fast and Slow by Nobel Prize winner Daniel Kahneman is a groundbreaking tour of the mind that explains the two systems that drive the way we think. System 1 is fast, intuitive, and emotional; System 2 is slower, more deliberative, and more logical. If you enjoyed this, check out our other psychology books.

This international bestseller offers profound insights into how choices are made in both our business and personal lives. Drawing on decades of research, Kahneman reveals where we can and cannot trust our intuitions and how we can tap into the benefits of slow thinking.

"Nothing in life is as important as you think it is when you are thinking about it." This book will change the way you think about thinking, offering practical and enlightening insights into how choices are made in both business and personal life.
