About the Book

The Galveston Diet: The Doctor-Developed, Patient-Proven Plan to Burn Fat and Tame Your Hormonal Symptoms is a revolutionary approach specifically designed for women experiencing hormonal changes. This comprehensive guide provides a science-backed plan to lose weight, balance hormones, and reclaim vitality during perimenopause, menopause, and beyond.

Developed by medical professionals and proven by thousands of patients, this diet addresses the unique metabolic and hormonal challenges women face. Learn how to combat stubborn belly fat, reduce inflammation, balance hormones naturally, and achieve sustainable weight loss without deprivation.
