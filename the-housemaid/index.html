About the Book

The Housemaid is a heart-pounding psychological thriller that will keep you guessing until the final page. When a young woman takes a job as a live-in housemaid for the wealthy Winchester family, she thinks she's found the perfect escape from her troubled past. But behind the pristine facade of their luxurious home lies dark secrets that threaten to consume her.

As she uncovers the family's disturbing truths, she realizes that the biggest threat isn't the mysterious occurrences in the house—it's the people who live there. Perfect for fans of psychological thrillers, this novel will have you questioning every character's motives until the shocking conclusion.

With masterful pacing and twists that will leave you breathless, The Housemaid has earned its place as a modern classic in the thriller genre, captivating over 187,000 readers worldwide.
